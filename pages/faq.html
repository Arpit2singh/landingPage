🔒 Security and privacy

On managed cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP) in primary regions, with encrypted backups. We can offer EU data residency for enterprise plans on request.

We never see your LinkedIn password. We use LinkedIn OAuth 2.0. The access and refresh tokens we receive are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and KMS-managed keys, and are never logged in plain text.

Our AI providers (OpenAI and Anthropic) are contractually prohibited from using your content to train their public models. Internally, we log only metadata about each run (model name, prompt template version, token usage and a quality-check verdict).

🔧 Troubleshooting

Open the post, we show the exact reason for failure. If it was a temporary network or LinkedIn issue, we already retried three times. If your LinkedIn token expired, reconnect it from Settings → Connected Platforms and reschedule.

LinkedIn access tokens expire periodically. Click Reconnect, it takes about ten seconds — and your scheduled posts will resume.

Iterate in chat. Try ‘shorter’, ‘more human’, ‘change the angle’, or ‘restart’. If the voice is consistently off, refresh your voice samples in Settings → AI Persona → Voice Training. Two or three new samples can change a lot.

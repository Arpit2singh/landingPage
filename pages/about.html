About publie.ai

Your LinkedIn voice, published consistently.

You know LinkedIn drives trust, leads and hiring. You also know you don't post enough. Manual writing eats an hour. Generic AI sounds like generic AI. And the tools that exist are either built for influencers chasing virality or priced for someone else's market.

publie.ai is built for you - who serve them. Set your AI Persona once. Chat with an AI agent that writes in your voice. Approve one polished draft. Publish it now or schedule it safely. That’s it.

10.1 Our Mission

To give every professional a reliable way to show up on LinkedIn in their own voice, without the blank page, without the robot tone and without ever giving up control of what gets published.

10.2 Our Vision

A LinkedIn where the best ideas reach the right people, written by the humans who actually have them - not drowned out by generic AI noise.

10.3 Our Objective

Help you publish at least 3 thoughtful posts a week, in your own voice, with an explicit approval gate on every single one - India-priced, global-ready, and built to protect your credibility before it chases your reach.
